01 The address

A modern oasis in a storied landed enclave.

Belgravia Ace is the final phase of the Belgravia freehold landed collection. Located off Ang Mo Kio Avenue 5, it brings together the privacy and scale of a landed home with the thoughtful amenities and security of a gated community.

Its name takes inspiration from London’s distinguished Belgravia district—an address known for grand homes, quiet streets and enduring prestige.
